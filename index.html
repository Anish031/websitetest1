About Us

Who We Are

Eminot Lifesciences is driven by a team of dedicated and highly qualified professionals committed to expanding the company's market presence while ensuring complete customer satisfaction.

We are a trusted supplier of world-class scientific laboratory products and instruments, serving a wide range of sectors across Karnataka. Our consistent focus on quality, reliability, and timely delivery has been instrumental in the company's steady growth.

Eminot Lifesciences was established by Prabhakaran S, a seasoned professional with more than 15 years of experience in the life sciences domain. Founded in 2023, we are committed to delivering quality scientific solutions.

Our Vision

To be the first choice for customers across India for reliable scientific laboratory products and solutions.

Our Mission

To establish Eminot as a globally recognized organization with a strong market presence and a growing clientele.

Our Services

Scientific Services

End-to-end services to support your research and development goals.

🧫

Assay Development

End-to-end assay development services customized for your research or diagnostic requirements. Our team designs, optimizes, and validates assays.

🔁

Custom Amplification

Specialized PCR amplification services with high fidelity and precision. We handle both standard and difficult-to-amplify sequences with expert care.

🧬

Custom Protein Expression

Recombinant protein expression and purification using bacterial, yeast, insect, and mammalian expression systems tailored to your specifications.

🔬

Gene Synthesis

High-fidelity gene synthesis for research, therapeutic, and industrial applications with codon optimization and full-sequence verification.

🧪

Oligonucleotide Design & Synthesis

Custom oligo design and synthesis for PCR, sequencing, CRISPR, and gene expression applications. Modifications and fluorescent labeling available.

📊

Sanger Sequencing

Accurate and rapid Sanger sequencing services for DNA sequence confirmation, mutation analysis, and vector verification with fast turnaround.
